What's the Name of Indian Manga or Comic Book?

Introduction to Indian Manga

Manga is a style of comic or graphic novel that originated in Japan, but its influence has reached every corner of the world, including India. In India, while the concept of Manga is not deeply rooted in tradition, there are creative individuals and teams trying to bring Indian stories to life through Manga-inspired styles.

Title for Indian Manga

The Title used for Indian comic books is "Manova".

Different Terms for Comics in diff countries

Manga, manhwa, and manhua are terms for comics from Japan, Korea, and China, respectively, each reflecting unique cultural styles and traditions. Manga are Japanese comics or graphic novels, read from right to left, covering diverse genres and deeply rooted in Japanese pop culture. Manhwa, originating from Korea, are often presented as webtoons with vertical scrolling and are read left to right, known for their vibrant art and modern storytelling. Manhua, from China, incorporate traditional Chinese aesthetics and are read left to right or vertically, gaining popularity through online platforms worldwide.
